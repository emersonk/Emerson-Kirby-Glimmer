My name is Emerson & I'm a computer science student at UBCO.

I enjoy creating elegent, succinct solutions for difficult problems that are just as comfortable in the dirt as they are on the wall. I've not yet found something in CS that I don't enjoy, but I will say I have a certain inclination for web dev and anything OOP at this point. I organized the team that competed in the ACM programming contest for UBCO this year and last year, which was great fun. I'm currently working at CoreHealth Technologies here in Kelowna as a junior developer.
